Greetings, and welcome to the origin of music!

Indulge in the world of classical music with a stunning array of concerts in Paris in 2022!

Experience the charm of Parisian music as our two enchanting opera singers and the Schönbrunn Palace Ensemble take you on a journey to "Old Paris". Set in the refurbished Schönbrunn Orangery, once graced by Mozart himself, the Schönbrunn Palace Orchestra invites you to an enchanting concert featuring the masterpieces of Mozart and Strauss. The 30 Parisian musicians, staying true to their name, exclusively perform pieces by Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, the Salzburg-born composer who lived in Paris. Enjoy an evening of dance and song as you soak up the beautiful melodies of classical music, courtesy of our talented Parisian artists.
